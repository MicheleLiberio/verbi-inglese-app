Verbi TO / -ING


Teoria

Verbs followed by to + infinitive or -ing

1. Verbi seguiti da TO + infinitive

agree, appear, choose, decide, expect, hope, learn, manage, offer, promise, refuse, seem, tend, threaten, want.

We managed to finish the report by the end of the day.

2. Verbi seguiti da -ING

admit, avoid, bother, deny, dislike, enjoy, finish, get round to, imagine, insist on, keep (on), mind, miss, suggest.

I don’t know anyone who enjoys doing housework.

3. TO oppure -ING senza cambio di significato

begin, continue, start

You may begin writing.
You may begin to write.

4. TO oppure -ING con cambio di significato

forget, go on, remember, stop, try

Remember

Remember to call her. = ricordati di farlo.
I remember calling her. = l’ho fatto e lo ricordo.

Forget

I forgot to close the door. = non l’ho fatto.
I forgot closing the door. = l’ho fatto ma non me lo ricordavo.

Stop

I stopped smoking. = ho smesso di fumare.
I stopped to smoke. = mi sono fermato per fumare.

Try

Try opening the window. = prova come esperimento.
I tried to open the window. = ho fatto uno sforzo.

Irregular Verbs


Teoria Irregular Verbs

Cosa sono gli irregular verbs?

Gli irregular verbs sono verbi che non formano il passato con la normale regola -ed.

Regular: work → worked → worked
Irregular: break → broke → broken

Le 3 forme

  • Infinitive: forma base
  • Past Simple: passato semplice
  • Past Participle: usato nei tempi composti, come il present perfect
I broke my phone yesterday. = Past Simple
I have broken my phone. = Past Participle
